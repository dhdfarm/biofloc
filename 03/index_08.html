Determination of Chemical Composition and Odorants

Proximate composition analysis of diet and fish was performed according to the standard methods described by AOAC (2003). Water samples for off-flavor analysis were centrifuged in 100-mL glass centrifuge tubes at 4 °C and 4000 rpm for 20 min to remove undissolved substance and biofloc.

The concentrations of geosmin and 2-MIB in water samples were determined by headspace solid-phase microextraction- gas chromatography–mass spectrometry (HSPME-GC–MS) and performed according to the methods described by Zuo et al. (2010).

The concentrations of odorants in fish filet were determined by microwave mediated distillation with HSPME-GC–MS determination (Zhu et al., 1999). The concentrations of geosmin and 2-MIB in the water and filet were analysed in duplicate for each sample.
