Illumina MiSeq Sequencing

Purified amplicons were pooled in equimolar and paired-end sequenced (2Ã—300) on an Illumina MiSeq platform (Illumina, San Diego, USA), according to the standard protocols by Majorbio Bio-Pharm Technology Co. Ltd. (Shanghai, China).

The raw reads were deposited into the NCBI Sequence Read Archive (SRA) database (Accessions: SRP156552 and PRJNA484590).
