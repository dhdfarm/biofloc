Overview of Bacterial Community Sequencing

In total, after denoising, 1,197,839 high-quality 16S sequences were obtained from 27 samples: on average, each sample had 50,654 sequences from the suspended solid in water, 41,480 sequences from the intestines of Gibel carp and 40,959 from the intestines of common carp.

After subsampling each sample to an even depth (25,666 reads per sample) and clustering, 2126 OUT at 97% identity were obtained. Overall, bacterial sequences belonged to 42 Phyla, 88 different Class, and 704 Genera.
