Experimental Facilities

To investigate the effect of BFT on water quality and fish production, we studied three treatments: CT, BT and BZT. Nine enclosures (three treatments in triplicate) were placed in an outdoor aquaculture pond to simulate a real aquaculture environment.

The square enclosures had five faces (bottom and surround, 2m3) made by tarpaulin sheets that prevented water exchange. Nine nylon net cages (2m3) were placed into the enclosures to rear the fish over a period of 8 weeks.

The upper edge of the enclosures and net cages were suspended 30 cm above the surface using a metal frame and plastic floats to keep the shape of the enclosures. The deep of pond were 1.7 m.

The water in the enclosures originated from the pond. The pond was located at the farm of National Protospecies Farm of Four Major Chinese Carp (29°49′48.8″N, 112°29′01.3″E) in Shishou, China, and had a surface area of 1960m2, which was standard and common in China.
