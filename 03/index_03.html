Experimental Fish and Diet

All experimental protocols including animal care were approved by the ethics committee of the Institute of Hydrobiology, Chinese Academy of Sciences.

Each enclosure was placed 30 Gibel carp (Carassius auratus gibelio, initial body weight 15 g) and 6 common carp (initial body weight 63.33 g) and assigned to different treatment randomly.

The experimental Gibel carp and common carp were obtained from the breeding farm of the Institute of Hydrobiology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. All fish were acclimatized and selected according to the standard procedure of our lab (Liu et al., 2016).

Diets were formulated containing crude protein levels of 32.0 g kg−1 (nitrogen was 5.12 g kg−1), crude lipid level of 8.0 g kg−1.
