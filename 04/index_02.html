Experimental Design

Biofloc produced in indoor tanks was used as dietary supplement in shrimp feed, over a 60-day indoor growth trial.

A control diet without biofloc was compared against three experimental diets with graded level of biofloc inclusion.

The experiment was conducted at Kakdwip Research Centre, Central Institute of Brackishwater Aquaculture, Kakdwip (21°51′N and 88°11′ E),West Bengal, India.
